About

Hello!

I’m Deb, outdoorsy animal lover.

I have been around animals my whole life from childhood dogs, rabbits and guinea pigs to teenage horses and my own dogs when I had a family, recently acquiring a tortoise!

I have been walking dogs professionally since November 2019.

Why Choose Woodbridge Wags and Walks?

I understand you can’t always give your dog the walks he or she needs, circumstances change.

I'm here to help.

Peace of Mind

I aim to put your mind at rest and take your dog out for new smells and adventure.

In a small group of four I can give your dog the attention they need and we are not too limited to where we can go safely.

Insured and Qualified

Fully insured and have Canine First aid Level 2 (VTQ)

I carry a basic first aid kit and all necessary details of your vet just in case.

Dogs are Matched

I will always match dogs on particular walks up, some nervous dogs need to walk on their own first to get to know me before being introduced to a few new friends.

Homemade dog treats

I make liver based treats, I always walk with a few in my pocket. Ingredients are liver, flour, eggs and a tiny bit of garlic. Its baked and chopped into small squares, freezes well and keeps in an airtight container in the fridge for 3-4 days.

Ideal for training or just a healthy homemade treat.

Bags of 200g are £3 or two bags for £5.
